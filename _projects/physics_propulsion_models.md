Developed a suite of physics-based plant models in MATLAB that replaced empirical estimates as the design authority for eVTOL development at VTOL Aviation India.

**Models built**

- State-space propulsion model
- Motor torque-speed curves across operating voltage and temperature
- Battery discharge models under varying load profiles
- Coupled airframe aerodynamics for propulsion-airframe interaction

**Flight envelope analysis**

- Conducted analysis across 50+ configurations
- Established control operating points and gain scheduling regions
- Converged on motor, battery, and drivetrain selections meeting range, payload, and redundancy requirements

**Calibration**

Models were systematically calibrated against data from the in-house 1 kHz propulsion test stand, achieving prediction error within 3% across the full envelope.
