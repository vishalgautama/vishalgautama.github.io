Led the complete development of a 500 lb payload-class heavy-lift multirotor UAV at VTOL Aviation India, from initial sizing through autonomous flight operations.

**Scope of work**

- **Sizing & design:** Motor selection, frame sizing, battery and drivetrain configuration meeting range, payload, and redundancy requirements
- **Avionics integration:** Full sensor and compute stack integration
- **GNC commissioning:** Control law development, gain scheduling, and HITL verification before first flight
- **Verification & test:** Eigenvalue stability analysis, Bode gain/phase margin assessment across the full operational envelope; authored 100+ V&V test cases with direct requirement traceability
- **Autonomous trajectory execution and first autonomous flight**

**Process rigor**

All control laws were verified through HITL bench testing before the platform left the ground. This established the acceptance criteria that formally cleared the platform for autonomous operations and reduced the risk of in-flight failures during the first autonomous flight campaign.
